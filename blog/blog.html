June 1 2018 - Where did Monday Go?

Communications Management - Craig

This week I completed the first iteration of the Communications Management onboarding and approval process mockup. This functionality will allow our customers to self-enroll in the various reports we offer and provide valuable visibility into their devices, usage and cost. On Friday I met with the Amigos and discussed the mockup in detail. Several impactful decisions were made which will result in the need to go back and create a second iteration of the management mockup. The primary changes to be made are the custom email page. In the first iteration we had only two choices and required a waiting period, the second iteration has three options and no waiting period. You can find the first iteration mockup of that page at: Custom Email Page, Iteration One and the second iteration of the page at: Custom Email Page, Iteration Two. More changes to come early next week!
